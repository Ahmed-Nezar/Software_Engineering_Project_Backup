Educapedia

Where Education is Fun




Our Vision:

Providing students with a comprehensive and engaging online learning experience that is aligned with the CIE IGCSE syllabus. The platform will offer courses in different subjects, multimedia content, downloadable study materials, and tools such as a virtual classroom and progress tracking. The platform will also provide supplementary services such as tutoring, study groups, and exam preparation courses. The goal is to create a world-class online learning platform that is accessible, affordable, and effective, enabling students to excel in their academic pursuits and beyond.

      


About

Educapedia has been founded in December 2021 by its six co-founders whom did not merely devoted their high school years in working with top tier IGCSE teachers from all subjects , but they were capable of also becoming achievers in most of subjects they took and for that , they are now presenting educpedia to their fellow students to aid them through their IGSCE journey by providing them with courses given by previous achievers as well as assisting them with sheets , quizzes , classwork , parent follow up , exams as well as providing them with well trained assistants who are available 24 hours
